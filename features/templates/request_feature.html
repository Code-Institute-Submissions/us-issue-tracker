Request New Feature

Requesting a new feature is free, however, a sufficient amount of funding will be required to go towards the development of this new feature.

Funding for the development process can be achieved through upvotes. Members can upvote a feature for a set fee of £20 per upvote. Once donated funds have reached £300, and there are at least 12 upvotes, the development process for the new feature will begin, and any more upvotes after this will also contribute to the making of this feature.

Here at Unicorn Simulator Issue Tracker, we promise to dedicate at least 50% of our time working on the highest paid feature.

You can keep up to date with the development processes on requested features by subscribing to our newsletter, or simply by checking in with the Requested Features tab.

Please be as detailed as possible when describing your requested feature to us. If we require any more information from you, we will not hesitate to contact you.
